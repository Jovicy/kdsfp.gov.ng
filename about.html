About Us

The Kaduna School Feeding Program, a flagship initiative of the state government, was launched in as a response to the vital link between nutrition and academic performance. Recognizing the significant impact that a balanced diet has on cognitive development, the program strives to break down barriers to education by addressing the nutritional needs of schoolchildren.

Our Mission:

We are dedicated to enhancing the educational experience and overall well-being of children in Kaduna by ensuring access to nutritious meals, fostering a conducive learning environment, and promoting community engagement.

Key Objectives:

Nutritional Empowerment: We believe in the transformative power of nutrition. By providing daily nutritious meals, we empower children to thrive academically and physically.

School Attendance: The program actively contributes to increased school attendance. By making schools a hub for nourishment, we create a compelling incentive for children to attend regularly.

Community Involvement: We foster a sense of community by involving local suppliers, farmers, and volunteers. This not only supports the local economy but also strengthens the bond between schools and their surrounding communities.

Health and Wellness: Our commitment to the well-being of the students extends beyond the classroom. By promoting healthy eating habits, we aim to instill a lifelong appreciation for nutrition and wellness.

Local Sourcing: We prioritize sourcing ingredients locally, supporting farmers and businesses within the community.

Balanced Meals: Our menu is designed to meet the nutritional needs of growing children, ensuring a balanced intake of proteins, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals.

Collaboration: We collaborate with schools, parents, and local authorities to create a comprehensive network dedicated to the success of the program.
